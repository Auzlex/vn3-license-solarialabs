## Short summary of key rules

The list below is a concise summary only. The Japanese PDF is the legal source.

- **Who may use:**  
  - Personal use: allowed (commercial or non-commercial).  
  - Business/corporate use: contact the rights holder for permission.

- **Uploading to online platforms:**  
  - Upload for your own use on social or game platforms: allowed.  
  - Upload to let other users use the asset on the platform: **not allowed**.

- **Sensitive content:**  
  - Sexual and violent expressions: allowed.  
  - Political or religious activity: **not allowed** (private use is not prohibited).

- **Modifying and outsourcing work:**  
  - Adjusting and modifying files: allowed.  
  - Outsourcing modification work between users: allowed.  
  - You may use parts as bases to modify other data: allowed.

- **Redistribution / distribution:**  
  - Redistributing the original data (unchanged): **not allowed**.  
  - Distributing modified versions: **not allowed**.

- **Media & software use:**  
  - Use in videos, streams, broadcasts, and publications: allowed.  
  - Embedding in software (game builds etc.) is allowed if the asset is not easily extractable.  
  - Physical goods (merch): contact the rights holder.

- **Derivative / fan works:**  
  - Reusing meshes/weights to make clothing or avatar parts: allowed, and distribution of those works is permitted under the rules for those specific cases.  
  - Creating works that merely *use the asset as a motif* (i.e., motif-based derivatives): distribution of those motif-based derivatives is **not allowed**.

- **Credit & transfer:**  
  - Credit is not required but appreciated.  
  - Transfer of rights or assignment of the license: **not allowed**.

- **Enforcement & notes:**  
  - Unauthorized redistribution may result in DMCA action. Provenance tracking is embedded.
